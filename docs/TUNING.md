# Tuning

## Aggressiveness

- 0 → no price control  
- 1–2 → mild  
- 3–4 → balanced  
- 5 → aggressive  

---

## Inertia

- Low → fast system  
- High → slow system  

Typical:

- Apartment → low  
- House → medium  
- Heavy house → high  

---

## Key idea

- Price decides WHEN  
- Inertia decides HOW EARLY
